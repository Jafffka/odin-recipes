Lasagna is a classic Italian pasta dish that has become a beloved comfort food around the world. It is a layered casserole made with wide flat pasta noodles, rich marinara sauce, creamy béchamel sauce, flavorful ground meat such as beef or pork, and a generous sprinkling of cheese, typically mozzarella, Parmesan, and ricotta. The dish is assembled by layering the pasta sheets with the meat sauce and béchamel, creating a hearty and satisfying meal. Lasagna is then baked until the top becomes golden and bubbly, resulting in a delightful harmony of textures and flavors. Each bite offers a perfect combination of tender pasta, savory meat, tangy tomato sauce, and the indulgent creaminess of the cheeses. It is often enjoyed as a centerpiece at family gatherings and celebrations, making it a timeless favorite that brings people together over a hearty and comforting dining experience.
